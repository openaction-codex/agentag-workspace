# Report template

Adapt the sections to the available evidence. Omit irrelevant rows, not
important unknowns.

```markdown
# Sales account brief: [organization]

Researched on [date] · Entity confidence: [High/Medium/Low]

## 90-second brief

**Organization:** [one or two sentences]

**OpenAction fit: [Strong/Plausible/Weak/Unknown]** — [decisive evidence and
any disqualifier]

**Why now:**
- [dated signal and why it matters]
- [dated signal and why it matters]

**Likely buying situation:** [concise supported interpretation, clearly marked
as an inference]

**Best contact:** [name, role, and account-specific reason; or exact target role
if no named person is verifiable]

**Recommended next action:** [one action and why]

## Account snapshot

| Dimension | Finding | Status / confidence |
|---|---|---|
| Entity and geography | ... | Confirmed fact · High |
| Mission and audience | ... | ... |
| Scale and structure | ... | ... |
| Financial capacity | ... | ... |
| Digital operating model | ... | ... |

## Digital estate

| Function | Observed tool or approach | Evidence strength | Sales relevance |
|---|---|---|---|
| Website/CMS | ... | ... | ... |
| CRM/member data | ... | ... | ... |
| Email/SMS | ... | ... | ... |
| Forms/events/donations | ... | ... | ... |
| Analytics/consent | ... | ... | ... |

State important blind spots, especially server-side systems that public
research cannot observe.

## Sales hypotheses to validate

### 1. [Hypothesis]
- **Evidence:** ...
- **Confidence:** ...
- **Would be disproved if:** ...
- **Ask:** "[one open discovery question]"

[Repeat for no more than three hypotheses.]

## Ideal contacts

| Rank | Person and current title | Likely buying role | Why this person | Confidence | Best verified professional route |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | ... | Problem owner / champion / evaluator / sponsor | ... | ... | [Official route](URL) |
| 2 | ... | ... | ... | ... | ... |
| 3 | ... | ... | ... | ... | ... |

For each named person, cite recent evidence of the current role and add one
recommended conversation angle. Include a credible partner or warm route when
one is evidenced. Do not force three names: when no reliable person is found,
name the target role/team and official organizational route instead. Never
guess an email address or include private contact details.

## Suggested opening

> [Short human-review draft based on one relevant professional signal.]

[If personal data is used: Apply OpenAction's prospect-information, lawful-
basis, opt-out, and suppression procedures before sending.]

## Risks and unknowns

- [Material uncertainty, contradiction, disqualifier, or research gap]

## Sources

- [Source title](direct URL) — supports [claim]; published/updated [date or
  unknown], accessed [date].
```

Keep citations adjacent to claims in the brief when practical, even when the
full source register is also included.
